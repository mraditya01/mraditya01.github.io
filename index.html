🔬 Research

Sound Localization and Classification for Self-driving Vehicle

Multiple Moving Agents Systems with physical multiple moving agents will be more and more common, e.g., automated warehouse, delivery service with robots, self-driving cars, etc. Obviously, such systems should be well-coordinated, but in general, coordination/cooperation themselves are ambiguous and intractable.

My research questions are twofold: 1) explore the possibility of sound localization on self-driving vehicle, 2) physics data simulation for audio in urban environment.

Keywords: Self-driving vehicle, Sound Localization and Classification, Physics Simulation

VR Teleoperation System with Real-Time 3D Reconstruction

Developed a VR-based teleoperation system that enables intuitive control of a robotic arm with real-time 3D environment reconstruction. The system integrates depth sensing and 3D reconstruction techniques to provide immersive spatial awareness in a human-in-the-loop VR environment.

The research focuses on improving teleoperation safety, responsiveness, and situational awareness by streaming reconstructed 3D scenes around the manipulator in real time, reducing latency while maintaining visual fidelity and control precision.

Keywords: VR Teleoperation, 3D Reconstruction, Robotic Arm, Human-in-the-Loop, Spatial Perception
